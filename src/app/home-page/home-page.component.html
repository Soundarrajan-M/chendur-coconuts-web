Our Products

Organic Dried Coconut

Made from fresh, organic coconuts sourced from certified sustainable farms. The coconut meat is carefully grated, dried, and processed to preserve its natural flavor and nutritional value. Free from preservatives, chemicals, and additives, it ensures a pure, natural product. Available in fine, medium, and coarse grades, this dried coconut is ideal for baking, confectionery, snacks, or cooking. Rich in fiber, healthy fats, and antioxidants, Organic Dried Coconut offers a long shelf life and is perfect for health-conscious consumers and the food industry.

Fully Husked Coconut

Carefully selected and processed to retain its freshness and natural goodness. Fully husked coconuts are perfect for culinary use, desserts, and traditional rituals. Known for their versatility, they are widely used in cooking, beverages, and as a natural source of hydration. Our fully husked coconuts are sourced responsibly and handled with care to meet the highest standards of quality.

Tender Coconut

Tender coconuts are a natural source of hydration and essential nutrients. The refreshing water is rich in electrolytes, making it an ideal beverage for a healthy lifestyle. The soft, delicate pulp is a delicious treat, enjoyed fresh or added to smoothies and desserts. At Chendur Coconut, we ensure our tender coconuts are harvested at the perfect time to deliver unmatched quality and taste.

Semi Husked Coconut

Semi-husked coconuts are ideal for both household and industrial use. With the outer husk partially removed, they offer convenience without compromising freshness. Perfect for cooking, ceremonies, or commercial purposes, these coconuts are known for their rich flavor and extended shelf life. Our semi-husked coconuts are processed with care to ensure consistent quality.
